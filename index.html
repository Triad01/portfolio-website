Projects

Flex9ja

Flex9ja is a web app that is built with the aim of giving users the ability to view different places where they could have a good relaxing time. It also gives authenticated users the ability to make uploads of places they have also visited which they will like to share with other users of the app and also make reviews.

Mapty

Mapty is a web application that allows users keep track of their workout session and also renders their current location on a map, workout session could include cycling or running. It also keeps track of users workout sessions and renders it back to them on a map when the uses visits the app again

Bankist app

Bankist app is a simple app that shows the layouts of a modern bank application, built with some advanced DOM manipulations

Forkify

Forkify is a web app built which enables users to make research of their favourite recipes and upload theirs too. It also authorizes users of an uploaded recipe to mark them as bookmarked. The forkify app also stores the state of users and their bookmarked recipes, hence upon subsequent use of the app, users bookmarked recipes are restored
